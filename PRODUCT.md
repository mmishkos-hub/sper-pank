# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Friends and viewers opening a playful, static imitation of a mobile banking interface, primarily on phones.

## Product Purpose

Provide a clearly fictional joke experience. Success means the interface is fun to explore while nobody can mistake it for a real bank or financial service.

## Brand Personality

Playful, obvious, and direct.

## Anti-references

The application must never present itself as a genuine bank without a prominent disclaimer. The warning must not obstruct controls or break the imitated interface.

## Design Principles

- Make the fictional nature unmistakable.
- Keep the disclaimer present without blocking interaction.
- Preserve the original interface structure for the joke to work.
- Prefer simple static behavior that remains reliable on GitHub Pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use explicit wording rather than color alone, maintain strong text contrast, and keep the warning readable on narrow mobile screens.
